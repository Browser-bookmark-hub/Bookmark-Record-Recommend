# Privacy Policy for Bookmark Record & Recommend

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-07

Thank you for choosing to use the "Bookmark Record & Recommend" Chrome Extension (hereinafter referred to as "the Extension"). We respect and protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we access, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## 1. Information We Access and Collect
The Extension requests certain browser permissions (such as `bookmarks`, `history`, `tabs`, `storage`, etc.) strictly to provide its core functionalities. 

- **Bookmarks:** Accessed to display, organize, and manage your browser bookmarks within the Extension interface.
- **Browsing History & Activity:** Accessed to analyze your reading times, track active time on specific sites, and provide intelligent bookmark and history recommendations.
- **Tab Information:** Accessed to manage opened links and prevent duplicate bookmark tabs from opening.

## 2. Local Storage and GitHub Cloud Sync
**We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on our own servers.** 

By default, all the data accessed by the Extension (including your bookmarks, history, reading statistics, and settings) is processed and saved entirely locally on your device using Chrome's built-in local storage (`storage.local` / `unlimitedStorage`). 

If you choose to use the "Push & Analyze" sync feature, you can authorize the Extension to push your bookmark records, recommendations, and Markdown analysis documents to your own personal GitHub repository. This transfer happens securely between your browser and the GitHub API. We never have access to this data, nor do we host any middleware servers.

## 3. How We Use the Information
The information accessed is used **exclusively** for the following single purposes:
- To visually display your bookmark trees and folders within your local browser.
- To calculate how much time you spend reading or viewing specific pages to build a localized recommendation algorithm.
- To allow you to manage, export, and calibrate your own usage data locally.
- To sync your records and AI analysis rules to your personal GitHub repository, if you explicitly enable and configure the GitHub integration.

## 4. Third-Party Access
Your data is never sent to our servers, and we do not and cannot share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information or browsing data with any third parties. 

If you enable the GitHub sync feature, your data will be transmitted to GitHub. Your data hosted on GitHub will be subject to GitHub's own privacy policy and security practices.

## 5. User Control and Data Deletion
You have complete control over your data. 
- You can clear the Extension's local data at any time either through the Extension's built-in settings or by uninstalling the Extension.
- Uninstalling the Extension will completely remove all associated data stored in your browser's local storage.
- You can delete your GitHub sync data by deleting the respective repository or files directly on GitHub.

## 6. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us via the Extension's support page or our GitHub repository issue tracker.
